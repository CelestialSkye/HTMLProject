General Information



The Bugatti The Black Car is a sports car supercar prototype concept car GT of luxury , the automaker French Bugatti . One of the most expensive and fast new cars in the world, sold for € 11 million excluding taxes, i.e. € 13.2 million with French VAT 1 , 2 , it is presented at the international motor show of Geneva 2019 , in tribute to the history of the legendary brand, and to the 110 years of its foundation in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti

The neo-retro-futuristic fastback bodywork in shiny carbon fiber is entirely handmade at the Bugatti factory in Molsheim in Alsace . The backbone of aluminum low roof, to the famous grille in horseshoe 13 reminds those of Bugatti and Bugatti Type SC57 Fireball Atlantic in 1930





This car is designed by designer Bugatti Étienne Salomé 5 (from the Royal College of Art in London ) 6 . It is inspired by Bugatti Veyron 16.4 (2005), Bugatti Chiron (2016), and Bugatti Divo (2018), as well as old Bugatti Aérolithe and Bugatti Type SC57 Atlantic from the 1930s , by Jean Bugatti (heir to Ettore Bugatti ) 7 . The main source of inspiration is the Bugatti Type SC57 Atlanticsince the goal of La Voiture Noire is to embody the values ​​of this car that has almost disappeared today



interior



Stephan Winkelmann, President of Bugatti describes the Bugatti La Voiture Noire interior as the most comfortable car ever produced. It is a coupe that feels like a limousine. The symmetrical layout and ergonomic controls will prevent anxiety when cruising at 250 mph. It took countless hours to combine inspiration from the past with the latest in safety and connectivity. Leather and machined metals are combined to create the perfect atmosphere for the owner. Nothing is left to chance, as the seats, pedals, and controls are measured and fitted to the owner.
